macOS Utility • Swift

Keep your Mac productive with a clock that's always where you need it.

Always-Clock is a lightweight macOS utility designed for deep-focus environments. It provides a persistent, highly customizable time overlay that integrates seamlessly with your native workflow.

View on GitHub arrow_outward

Built for macOS Performance.

Unlike heavy web-based widgets, Always-Clock is built with native Swift and AppKit to ensure zero impact on system resources. It respects your Mac's battery life and integrates perfectly with System Settings.
